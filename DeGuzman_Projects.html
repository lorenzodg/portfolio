Scratch Project | August 2018 | Aiden Manansala | Lorenzo De Guzman | CSP

Our task was to create a game or story in Scratch. We imediatly agreed on creating a story. Our tale was based on a human-like entity in another realm, attempting to find treasure inside of a old temple. The task inside the game was to choose options in order to find the lost treasure, awaiting inside the temple. In this project, we had a very trusted type partnership so there was no need for any type of role. We could bounce ideas back and forth to each other, discuss it, and then place the altercation into the story. We both displayed our strengths in creativity, effiency, and collaboration better tahn I thought we would.
